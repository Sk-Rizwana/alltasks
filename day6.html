About the prabhas

Born as Prabhas Raju Uppalapati, Prabhas is a popular Indian actor who appears in Telugu cinema

He is the nephew of the veteran actor of the Telugu film industry, Uppalapati Krishnam Raju.

In the year 2015, Prabhas starred in the movie, Baahubali, which is one of the highest grossing movies of Indian cinema and later reprised his titular role in Baahubali 2: The Conclusion (2017), which became the first Indian film in the history of Indian cinema
